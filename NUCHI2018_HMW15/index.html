Earthquake Dashboard

The map shown below was created using Leaflet with GeoJason Data found in the Earthquake Hazards Program from the last seven days. The data markers over the map reflect the magnitude of the earthquake in their size and color. Earthquakes with higher magnitudes appear larger and darker in color. Popups are included to show additional data. A second data set on the map to illustrates the relationship between tectonic plates and seismic activity.
Data is updated every day and you can find it in United States Geological Survey (USGS)
